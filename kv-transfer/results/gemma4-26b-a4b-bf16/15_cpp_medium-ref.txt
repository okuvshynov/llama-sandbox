### 1. Correctness & Critical Bugs

#### A. Use-After-Free in `main` (MemoryArena)
In `main()`, the code calls `arena.reset()`, which deletes all previously allocated blocks except the last one. Immediately after, it attempts to access `records[0]->tag`. Since `records[0]` points to memory that has been `delete[]`-ed, this is **Undefined Behavior (Use-After-Free)**.
*   **Fix:** Ensure all pointers owned by the arena are invalidated or the arena is not reset until the objects are no longer needed.
