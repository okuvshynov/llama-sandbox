### 1. `scheduler.go`

#### A. Bug: Infinite Recursion in `AssignTask`
**Issue:** In `AssignTask`, if the task popped from the heap is in the `TaskCancelled` state, the function calls itself recursively: `return s.AssignTask(workerID)`.
**Explanation:** If a user submits a large batch of tasks and then cancels them, or if the queue becomes filled with cancelled tasks, `AssignTask` will enter an infinite recursive loop, eventually causing a **stack overflow**.
**Fix:** Replace the recursion with a `for` loop.
